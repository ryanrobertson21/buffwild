Buying Instructions

Buff Wild Crew, Season One, Genisis Collection

All BUFFs are created equal.

Therefore, each BUFF NFT will be 100 XRD each for the entirety of the sale from the Season One Genisis Collection.

All BUFFs tokens will be randomized upon purchase. There will be NO cherry-picking BUFFs.

In the collection there are 9910 BUFFs that have been randomly generated from 6 categories of over 230 traits. In addition to the randomly generated BUFFs, there are 90 one of ones integrated into the minting process, to create a grand total of 10,000 Buff Wild NFT images.

150 BUFF Wild NFTs (which will be randomly purchased) will be held for marketing purposes and Wild Ticket Prizes.

There will be no BUFF Wild NFTs withheld from the collection for the Crew Council or Community Team. Each Crew Council or Community Team Member may purchase up to the unique wallet limit of 150 BUFFs at their own expense from their own personal funds. Each Crew Council and Community Team member has been given a unique buff, as can be seen in the Team section of the Home page, however these BUFFs are not part of the collection, and will never be listed for sale.

STEP ONE: Decide how many Buffs you would like to purchase. Each Buff Wild NFT is 100 XRD. There is a max wallet limit of 150 Buffs.

Trading Buff Wild NFTs:

DO NOT attempt to trade Buff Wild NFTs between each other before the Trading Platform has launched. Only the wallet that originally made the purchase will be recognized.

The trading platform will be uploaded to the BWC Website within a few of weeks of the initial launch of the Buff Wild Crew!

If you attempt to trade Buff Wild NFT(s) tokens beforehand you will run the risk of losing your tokens to the ether. Off market trades that are not sanctioned by the Buff Wild Crew Council will not be honored.

Once the trading platform is live, the Buff Wild Crew Council will retain a 10% royalty on all secondary market trades.
