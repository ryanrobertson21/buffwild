Remember the Buffalo.


In the North American plains, the Buffalo once roamed the land free, and their herds were over 30 million strong. Due to the American settlers migrating west, the Buffalo were hunted, shot down, killed for fun, and run down to near extinction. It is estimated that in 1884 the American Buffalo, Bison, numbers living in the wild were down to a staggering 325. Most experts at the time believed the Bison would become extinct in the wild.
